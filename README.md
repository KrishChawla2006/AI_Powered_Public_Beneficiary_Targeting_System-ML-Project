## AI_Powered_Public_Beneficiary_Targeting_System-ML-Project

# Solving Real World Problem for choosing households those are eligible to government schemes 
simultaneously with the help of Multilabel Classification.(Models used in comparision:XGBoost,Light GBM, Random Forst, Decision Tree, Logistic Regression, K Nearest Neighbor)
we have used 5 schemes such as:
1.Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY - Gramin)
2.National Food Security Act (NFSA)-Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY)
3.National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP)-Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme (IGNDPS)
4.Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)
5.Ayushman Bharat - Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY)
